Media Content

To provide as much information as possible to the QC department about the logical identification/content of the media ie. Program & Episode titles, Distributor Name etc.These information are mandatory for overall operation coordination between QC, Scheduling and Media Library departments. The Media Content subwindow is in display onlymode. The information is gathered from other modules of Gen21 and displayed for information purposes only. No record editing functions (add, modify, delete) are allowed at this point

Step Media Content

1.       Login to Gen21CMS.

2.       Open the technical (Menu-> Quality Control-> Technical).

3.       Press Button Media Content
