Attachment

                This is the attachment contains about Channel Affiliate.

 

Open the Attachment

1.    Login to Gen21CMS

2.    Open the Program Purchase Contract (Program Management-> program Purchase Contract)

3.    In search Tab, Press Search Button

4.    In Browse tab, Select the PO and Open the Form Tab

5.    Press Button Attachment

 

Steps Adding New Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Create new Attachment,

                a. Navigate to Form Tab and click Insert button to create new Attachment

                b. Define the ID, Remarks and Attachment

                c.  Click Save button.

 

Steps Editing Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Edit specific Attachment,

                a. on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

                b. Edit the ID, Remarks and Attachment

                c. on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Delete Attachment,

                a. on mouse button and choose “edit mode”

                b. on mouse button and choose “delete”

                c. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
