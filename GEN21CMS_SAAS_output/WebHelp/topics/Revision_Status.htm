Revision Status

To list down all 'Revisions' of the media. Pls refer to Gen21 Quality Control  for background information on 'Revisions'. Available to view are the revision's summary status screen and detailed Technical QC and Program Cue Sheet pages of the revision .

Steps Revision Status   

1.      Click on the Revision Status menu item in the left vertical subwindow menu. The information is displayed by clicking any one of the 3 tabs at the top of the page (Please observe the titlenames on the tabs). The summary status screen of the revision is as shown here:

2.      There may be multiple lines appearing here depending on how many times 'Revisions' have been performed on the media by authorized persons. Each revision is displayed as a single line with clear numbering scheme starting from 0. To view detailed information of a particular revision, appropriately select the revision line on the screen and click on individual tabs to view either the Technical QC Form or the Program Cue Sheet of that revison.

On each one may re-print the pages as desired, but please note that all screens are in display only mode. No record editing of any form are allowed at this point.
