Censorship 

         Purpose   

        To show the episode's censorship detail informations.

         Steps to proceed:

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Censorship menu item. 

2.      To add new Censorship, right click at the Censorship section and select Edit,right click at the Censorship section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button .

3.      To modify Censorship, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Censorship section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button .

4.      To delete a Censorship, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Censorship section and select Edit, right click again at the Censorship section and select delete.  

       Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
