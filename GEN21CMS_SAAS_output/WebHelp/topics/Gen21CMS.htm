Gen21CMS

Gen21 Content Management System is an important functional extension to the Gen21 Broadcasting Management System (BMS) that focuses on the operational/management aspects of handling a broadcaster's program material inventory. The system streamlines all media handling activities from purchase to library, through accounts/financial, film management and program airing, to quality control and material circulation.

Gen21 Content Management System is capable of handling all related inventory operations of Acquisition, Media Library and Quality Control user departments, as well as maintaining the highest degree of integration to all components of the Gen21 BMS suite of products (such as the traffic-related functions of Scheduling, Sales and Accounting departments).

 

 

Please notice the module interdependency nature of the application and their interaction points with the Master Control.

Overall, this system provides a ready to use system that minimizes the learning curve through the use of a user-friendly interface, easy setup, speedy implementation and comprehensive documentation.
