Program Management

The Program Management Module of Gen21 Inventory specifically deals with most of the common tasks of the Acquisition (Programming) department, which not only centred around the process of Acquiring the broadcasting material itself but also the logical maintenance activities on the acquired material (eg. status monitoring of contract paperwork, License/Run/Rights/Sharing entitlements, Budgeting, Costing, Amortization etc).

Also included in the module are typical Programming department functionalities of Content handling (eg. the entry of content-related information like Synopsis, Stars, Categorization, Ratings etc.), and Information Analysis/Reporting. Various standard reports associated with these tasks are available as well as a number of standard, flexible built-in formatting features applicable to each report like Sorting, various Format Saving and Filtering. 

As a standard feature common to every Gen21 suite of products module, the Program Management module utilizes extensive Parameterization features incorporated into its Master definition facilities that not only minimizes key strokes and human errors but also ensures optimum data integrity across database tables and user interface. 

The Program Management Module of Gen21 Inventory is also suitable for use by those departments that produces the broadcasting material (eg. In-House and News departments) to enter all their material-related information for access by the whole organization, and therefore enabling them to get all the benefits of every utilization, maintenance, reporting functionalities and total information integration provided by Gen21 suite of products.

Accessing the Program Management Menu

  1. Open the Gen21CMS
  2. Click on Program Management Menu
