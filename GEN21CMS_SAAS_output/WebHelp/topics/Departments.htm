Departments

Used to Creating the Departments definition. It divides users/media borrowers based on their functionality.

Open the Departments

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Departments menu (Media Library => Parameters => Departments) 

 

Steps Adding Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Create new Departments,

 

a. Define the Dept/Area Name, Dept/Area Head, Dept/Area Email, Level, Subordinate of and Blank Tape Dept

b.    Click “Save” button

 

Steps Editing Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Edit specific Departments,

a. Edit the Dept/Area Name, Dept/Area Head, Dept/Area Email, Level, Subordinate of and Blank Tape Dept

b. Click “Save” button.

 

Steps Deleting Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Delete Departments,

a. On Browse Tab, select the appropriate Departments.

b. Double click on Departments or Navigate to Form Tab and click Edit button.

c. Click Delete button.

d. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
