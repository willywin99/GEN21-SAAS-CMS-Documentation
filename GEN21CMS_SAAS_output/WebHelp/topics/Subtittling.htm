Subtittling

            Purpose   

         To add and delete episode's subtitling process detail information.

           Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Subtitling menu item.

2.      To add new SubTitling, right click at the SubTitling section and select Edit or Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button. 

3.      To modify SubTitling, select the media from the browse list, right click at the SubTitling section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button . 

4.      To delete a SubTitling, select the media from the browse list, right click at the SubTitling section and select Edit, right click again at the SubTitling section and select delete. 

        Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
