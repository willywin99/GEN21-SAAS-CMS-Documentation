Quality Control

The Quality Control Module of Gen21 Inventory specifically deals with pre-airing material examination  activities of the Quality Control (QC) department , which mainly :

 

Examine and record details of the material in 2 major areas 'Technical' and 'Content' related. Rate specific and overall 'airing readiness status' of the material. These are represented by the values in the 'QC' and the 'Scheduling Status' Flags, collectively entered by the users as well as the system. Pls see system default behaviour below. Produce the necessary output forms to be used as guidance for airing. These typically are in the form of 'Technical QC Form' and the 'Program Cue Sheet' . To support day-to-day needs of the QC department, various standard reports associated with these tasks are available as  well as a number of standard, flexible built-in formatting features like Sorting, various Format Saving and Filtering.  As a standard feature common to every Gen21 suite of products module, the module utilizes extensive Parameterization features incorporated into its Master definition facilities that not only minimizes key strokes and human errors but also ensures optimum data integrity across database tables and user interface.

Open the Quality Control Menu

  1. Login to Gen21CMS.
  2. Open the Master Menu (Menu-> Quality Control ).
