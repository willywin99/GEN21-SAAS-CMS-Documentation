Working in synch with the Program / Episode Details and Scheduling functions, this is an easy facility to enter, apply and manage amortization schedule to programs / episodes in a  PO .  When applied, amortization schedule impacts on the material's 'current book value' in decreasing manner by a fixed value determined by the chosen amortization method (ie. 'by value' or 'by percentage'), and triggered by each 'Run' (airing) of the material across the amortization period.

Open the Amortization Schedule

1. Follow the steps outlined in Program Purchase Contractto get to the main Program Purchase Contract(Form) screen and click on the Amortization Schedule menu item.

2. Note: Make sure Programs and Episodes for the PO have been entered as outlined in Add Program.

3.  To add new Amortization Schedule, right click at Specification Schedule and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.

4. To delete Pattern in Amortization Schedule, right click at Specification Schedule and select Delete Pattern, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.

5.  Select the region for selected episode,means you also will done the amortization with region wise.
