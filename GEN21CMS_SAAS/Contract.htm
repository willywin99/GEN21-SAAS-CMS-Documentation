The 'Contract' - also called the 'PO Header', stores financial and administrative information of a PO.

Contract has following sub modules:

  1. Contract Info
  2. Purchage Details
  3. History
  4. Addendum
  5. Payment Schedule
  6. Amortization Schedule
  7. Channel Affiliate
  8. Attachment
  9. Child/parent PO
  10. Cancel PO
