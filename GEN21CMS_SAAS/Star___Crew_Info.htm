Used for creating and maintenance of Stars Info definition

 

Open the Star / Crew Info

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Primary Subjects (Menu-> Media Library -> Parameters -> Star / Crew Info)

 

Steps Adding Star / Crew Info

1. Open Star / Crew Info

2. Create new Star / Crew Info,

      a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose

          “add row” to create new Star / Crew Info

b. Define the Type, First Name, Last Name, Address, Email Address, Phone Number, Contact Person, Phone Number and Remarks

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Editing Star / Crew Info

1. Open Star / Crew Info

2. Edit specific Star / Crew Info,

      a.  Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

  b. Edit the Type, First Name, Last Name, Address, Email Address, Phone Number, Contact  Person, Phone Number and Remarks

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Star / Crew Info

1. Open Star / Crew Info

2. Delete Star / Crew Info,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”

      b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”
