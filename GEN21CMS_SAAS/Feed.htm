Used to Creating the Feed definition. this to add the Feed in Schedule for Recording.

Open the Feed

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Primary Subjects (Menu-> Media Library -> Parameters -> Feed)

 

Steps Adding Feed

1. Open Feed

2. Create new Feed,

      a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose

          “add row” to create new Feed

b. Define the Feed Code and Feed Name

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Editing Feed

1. Open Feed

2. Edit specific Feed,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

  b. Edit the Feed Name

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Feed

1. Open Feed

2. Delete Feed,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”

      b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”
