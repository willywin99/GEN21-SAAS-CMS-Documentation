Basic Setup for Month End

Access to Master Menu

Open the Month End  

1.                  Open the Gen21 CMS

2.                  Open the Month End (Menu-> Master-> Month End).

         

          

        Steps for Month End 

1.      Choose-> All Month End -> Search

2.      Press Mouse right button in Month End detail section for open the Popup window

3.      Enter the Month

4.      Enter the Year

5.      Enter the Close

Press Save from Main Menu
