Purpose

This is a common, general purpose and user-friendly maintenance facility for users who have less access rights to information (eg. Scheduling, Traffic or  Media Library department staff) to go directly to the detailed episode level screen that has the most frequently required information for them to view and make limited changes on.

Episode Details has following sub modules:

?         Dubbing

?         SubTitling

?         Synopsis

?         Stars Info

?         Awards

?         Attribute

?         Censorship

?         Tapes Info

?         Runs / Plays

?         Pre / Req

?         Episode Cost

?         Attachment

?         Target Audience

?         Song Info

          Please refer to Program Purchase Contract .
