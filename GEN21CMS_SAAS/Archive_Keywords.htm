Used to Creating the 'Archive Keywords' definition. These keywords will label stored media to describe its contents at a more detailed level in order to facilitate quicker access to the right media and its contents. Each media can be labeled with a maximum of 15 (fifteen) Archive Keywords (as shown in the Receive Program Media , in the 'assign archive keywords' button section).

Open the Archive Keywords

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Primary Subjects (Menu-> Media Library -> Parameters -> Archive Keywords)

 

Steps Adding Archive Keywords

1. Open Archive Keywords

2. Create new Archive Keywords,

      a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose

          “add row” to create new Archive Keywords

      b. Define the Key Type and Description

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Editing Archive Keywords

1. Open Archive Keywords

2. Edit specific Archive Keywords,

      a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

      b. Edit the Key Type and Description

      c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Archive Keywords

1. Open Archive Keywords

2. Delete Archive Keywords,

              a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode"

               b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”
