Purpose   

           To add and delete episode's awards detail informations.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Awards menu item. 

2.      To add new Awards, right click at the Awards section and select Edit, right click at the Awards section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.

3.      To modify Awards, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Awards section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button . 

4.      To delete a Awards, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Awards section and select Edit, right click again at the Awards section and select delete.

        Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
