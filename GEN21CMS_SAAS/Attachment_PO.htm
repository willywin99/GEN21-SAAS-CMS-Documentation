This is the attachment contains about Channel Affiliate.

 

Open the Attachment

1.    Login to Gen21CMS

2.    Open the Program Purchase Contract (Menu-> Program Management-> program Purchase Contract)

3.    In search Tab, Press Search Button

4.    In Browse tab, Select the PO and Open the Form Tab

5.    Press Button Attachment

 

Steps Adding New Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Create new Attachment,

                a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose

                 “add row” to create new Attachment

                b. Define the ID, Remarks and Attachment

                c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Editing Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Edit specific Attachment,

                a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

                b. Edit the ID, Remarks and Attachment

                c. Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Attachment

1. Open Attachment

2. Delete Attachment,

                a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”

                b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”
