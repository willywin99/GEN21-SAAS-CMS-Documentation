Display the Purchase detail report for printing depending upon selection criteria of the user.

 

Open the Program Purchase Details Report

1.    Login to Gen21CMS

2.    Open the Program Purchase Contract (Menu-> Program Management-> program Purchase Contract)

3.    In search Tab, Press Search Button

4.    In Browse tab, Select the PO and Open the Form Tab

5.    Press Button Purchase Details

6.    Purchase details are obtained from the purchase contract program transaction results
