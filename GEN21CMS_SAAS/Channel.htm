Used for creating and maintain of Channel.

How to open Channel

  1. Log in to Gen21 CMS.
  2. Open Channel menu (Program Management -> Parameters -> Channel).

How to Create Channel

  1. Open Channel menu.
  2. Navigate to Form Tab.
  3. Click on Insert button below Form tab or CTRL + i.
  4. Fill in the Form.
  5. Click Save or CTRL + S.

How to Delete Channel

  1. Open Channel menu.
  2. On Browse Tab, select the channel want to be delete.
  3. Navigate to Form Tab or double click on selected channel.
  4. Click Delete Button.
  5. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
