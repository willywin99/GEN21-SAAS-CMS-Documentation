Purpose   

        To add and delete episode's dubbing process detail information.

            Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Dubbing menu item.

2.      To add new Dubbing, right click at the Dubbing section and select Edit, right click at the Dubbing section again and select Add,enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button .

3.      To modify Dubbing, select the media from the browse list, right click at theDubbing section and select Edit,retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button.

4.      To delete a Dubbing, select the media from the browse list, right click at theDubbing section and select Edit,right click again at the Dubbing section and select delete.

         Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application
