Used to Creating the Purpose definition. These are self-defined codes & descriptions of purposes which will be used by Program Media Check-In/Out and Stock Shoot Check-In/Out .

Open the Purpose

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Purpose menu (Media Library => Parameters => Purpose). 

 

Steps Adding Purpose

1. Open the Purpose.

2. Create new Purpose,

    a. Navigate to Form Tab and click Insert button.

    b. Define the Purpose Description.

    c. Click Save button.

 

Steps Editing Purpose

1. Open the Purpose.

2. Edit spesific Purpose,

    a. On Browse Tab, select the appropriate Purpose.

    b. Double click on selected Purpose or Navigate to Form Tab and click Edit button.

    c. Edit the Purpose description.

    d. Click Save button.

 

Steps Deleting Purpose

1. Open the Purpose.

2. Delete specific Purpose,

    a. On Browse Tab, select the appropriate Purpose.

    b. Double click on selected Purpose or Navigate to Form Tab and click Edit button.

    c. Click Delete button.

    d. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
