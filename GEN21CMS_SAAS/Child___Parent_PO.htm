To make a PO as a child of another PO when, Another PO contents license of a progam and we want to make a PO for its episodes. We want to insert another episode from different PO into current episode. We want to extend current program by adding another episode from different PO.

Open the Child / Parent PO

1. Click on the Program Management >  Program Purchase Contract > Child/Parent PO menu item.

2. To add new Child/Parent PO, right click at the header and select Edit, right click at the header again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then  click the save button. 

3. To modify Child/Parent PO, select the media from the browse list, right click at the header of Form tab and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button .  

4. To delete a Child/Parent PO, select the media from the browse list, right click at the header and select Edit, right click again at the header and select delete.
