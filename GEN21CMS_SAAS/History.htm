Used to display the history report of Purchase details based on the revision number selected by the user.

Open the History Report

1.    Login to Gen21CMS

2.    Open the Program Purchase Contract (Menu-> Program Management-> program Purchase Contract)

3.    In search Tab, Press Search Button

4.    In Browse tab, Select the PO and Open the Form Tab

5.    Press Button History

6.    History can be obtained at the time of purchase contract program revision
