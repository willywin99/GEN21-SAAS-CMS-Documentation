Used to Creating the Departments definition. It divides users/media borrowers based on their functionality.

Open the Departments

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the Primary Subjects (Menu-> Media Library -> Parameters -> Departments)

 

Steps Adding Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Create new Departments,

a. Right click on mouse button on the header field and choose“add row” to create new Departments

b. Define the Dept/Area Name, Dept/Area Head, Dept/Area Email, Level, Subordinate of and Blank Tape Dept

c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Editing Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Edit specific Departments,

a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode”.

b. Edit the Dept/Area Name, Dept/Area Head, Dept/Area Email, Level, Subordinate of and Blank Tape Dept

c.  Right click on mouse button and choose “save”

 

Steps Deleting Departments

1. Open Departments

2. Delete Departments,

a. Right click on mouse button and choose “edit mode"

b. Right click on mouse button and choose “delete”
