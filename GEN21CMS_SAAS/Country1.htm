Used for creating and maintain Country.

Open Country Menu

  1. Login to GEN21 CMS.
  2. Open Country menu. (Program Management => Parameters => Country)

How to Add Country

  1. Open Country menu.
  2. Navigate to Form Tab.
  3. Click Insert button or CTRL + I.
  4. Entry Country Code.
  5. Entry Country Name.
  6. Entry PPH %.
  7. Entry PPN %.
  8. Entry SAP Code.
  9. Click Save button or CTRL + S.

How to Delete Country

  1. Open Country menu.
  2. On Browse Tab, select the Country which one to be delete.
  3. Navigate to Form Tab or double click on selected country.
  4. Click Edit button.
  5. Click Delete button.
  6. Click OK for confirm the deletion.
