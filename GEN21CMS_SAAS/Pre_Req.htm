Purpose  

          To display/maintain the episode's airing readiness status.

          This is determined by listing all applicable readiness elements of the episode and the status of each one.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Pre/Req menu item.

2.      To add new Pre/Reg, right click at the Pre/Reg section and select Edit, right click at the Pre/Reg section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button. 

3.      To modify Pre/Reg, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Pre/Reg section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button.

4.      To delete a Pre/Reg, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Pre/Reg section and select Edit, right click again at the Pre/Reg section and select delete.      

         Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
