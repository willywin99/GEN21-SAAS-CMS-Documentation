Purpose   

         To show the episode's runs/plays detail informations.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Runs/Plays menu item. 

2.      To add new Runs/Plays, right click at the Runs/Plays section and select Edit, right click at the Runs/Plays section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.   

3.      To modify Runs/Plays, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Runs/Plays section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button.              

4.      To delete a Runs/Plays, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Runs/Plays section and select Edit, right click again at the Runs/Plays section and select delete. 

                  Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application .
