Purpose   

          To add and delete episode's song detail information.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Song Info menu item.

2.      To add new Song Info, right click at the Song Info section and select Edit, right click at the Song Info section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button .

3.      To modify Song Info, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Song Info section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button .  

4.      To delete a Song Info, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Song Info section and select Edit, right click again at the Song Info section and select delete.  

5.      Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
