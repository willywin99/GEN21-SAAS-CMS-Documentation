Used for create and maintain of Distributor Details.

Open Distributor Menu

  1. Login to GEN21 CMS.
  2. Open Distributor menu. (Program Management => Parameters => Distributor)

 

How to Add Distributor

  1. Open Distributor menu.
  2. Navigate to Form Tab.
  3. Click Insert Button or CTRL + I.
  4. Fill in Distributor Master Form.
  5. Click Save Button or CTRL + S.

 

How to Add Agency to Distributor

  1. Open Distributor menu.
  2. On Browse Tab, select the appropriate Distributor.
  3. Navigate to Form Tab or double click on selected Distributor.
  4. On Agency Form click Add Button.
  5. Select the Agency which one to be added.
  6. Click Save or Insert (✔️) Button on left side column.

How to Add Channel to Distributor

  1. Open Distributor menu.
  2. On Browse Tab, select the appropriate Distributor.
  3. Navigate to Form Tab or double click on selected Distributor.
  4. On Channel Form click Add Button.
  5. Select the Channel which one to be added.
  6. Click Save or Insert (✔️) Button on left side column.

 

How to Delete Distributor

To Delete a Distributor please make sure that Agency and Channel that have been added to this distributor has been deleted.

  1. Delete Agency and Channel on this Distributor by click Remove Button on right side of each column (❌).
  2. Click Save Button.
  3. Click Delete Button for delete this Distributor.
  4. Click OK to confirm the deletion.
