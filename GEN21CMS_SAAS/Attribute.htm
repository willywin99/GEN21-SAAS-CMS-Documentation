Purpose  

          To add and delete episode's attribute detail information.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Attribute menu item 

2.      To add new Attribute, right click at the Attribute section and select Edit, right click at the Attribute section again and select Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button. 

3.      To modify Attribute, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Attribute section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button .  

4.      To delete a Attribute, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Attribute section and select Edit, right click again at the Attribute section and select delete.

       Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application.
