Used to enter all technical-related data of the media. Typically the first QC action to be done on a media during review, the data entry is done on this form containing a whole list of technical fields to be filled in . And when completed, the users are also expected to immediately fill in the 'Status' of the media (ie. the result of examination with regards to technical aspects of the media).

Step Technical QC Form

 

1. Login to Gen21CMS.

2. Open the technical (Menu-> Quality Control-> Technical).

3. Press Button Technical QC Form
