Purpose  

           To show the episode's physical tape detail informations.

Steps to proceed

1.      Click on the Program Management > Manage Episode Details > Episode Details > Tapes Info menu item. 

2.      To add new Tapes Info, right click at the Tapes Info section and select Edit or Add, enter the values in modifiable fields then click the save button.   

3.      To modify Tapes Info, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Tapes Info section and select Edit, retype over any modifiable fields and click the save button . 

4.      To delete a Tapes Info, select the media from the browse list, right click at the Tapes Info section and select Edit, right click again at the Tapes Info section and select delete.

        Note: Deletions are not possible when the records in question are already used in relation to any other entities in the application
